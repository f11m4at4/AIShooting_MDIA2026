EnemyExplosion generator entrypoint

Run this from a Unity 6000.x editor for this project:

- Menu: Tools/HO/Create Enemy Explosion Effect
- Batch mode: -executeMethod HO_CreateEnemyExplosionEffect.CreateAssets

Expected generated assets:

- Assets/Effects/EnemyExplosion.prefab
- Assets/Effects/EnemyExplosionSmoke.png
- Assets/Effects/EnemyExplosionBurst.png
- Assets/Effects/EnemyExplosionSmoke.mat
- Assets/Effects/EnemyExplosionBurst.mat

The generator creates an unlit particle effect with:

- a bright stylized flash
- a short additive burst
- expanding smoke
